## Data Quality Notes

1. **2009 correction**: Wikipedia and other sources show only 6 teams in 2009, not 8. Some sources incorrectly list Rochester as playing - they were on hiatus.

2. **2010 team count**: Chesapeake formed from Washington/Toronto merger. Some sources count this differently.

3. **2012 name change**: Long Island Lizards officially became New York Lizards on December 14, 2012 (before 2013 season).

4. **Rochester gaps**: Rochester Rattlers had two stints: 2001-2008 and 2011-2017, with a 2-year gap (2009-2010) and single year off (2016).

5. **Philadelphia gaps**: Philadelphia Barrage played 2004-2008, then returned for final season 2020 only.
